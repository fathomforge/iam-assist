# Code of conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

In short:

- **Be kind.** Assume good intent. Disagree with the idea, not the person.
- **Be specific.** Vague criticism ("this is bad") is not useful; point at
  the line and suggest an alternative.
- **Be patient.** Maintainers have day jobs. Contributors have day jobs.
  Review turnaround isn't going to be the same as a commercial product.
- **Don't bring fights here.** Keep politics, personal drama, and
  unrelated grievances out of issues and PRs.

Behaviors that will get you blocked without warning:

- Harassment or discriminatory language targeting any person or group
- Sharing private communications without consent
- Posting other people's personal information
- Sustained disruption of discussion

## Enforcement

Report violations to **conduct@fathomforge.dev**. Reports are handled by
the project maintainer. We will acknowledge within 7 days and describe any
actions taken.

The maintainer reserves the right to remove comments, close issues, block
accounts, and take any other action necessary to keep the project a
reasonable place to collaborate.
